Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

The Sharon Independence Day mobile application ("the App") is operated by the Sharon Independence Day Committee ("we," "us," or "our"), a volunteer community organization in Sharon, Massachusetts.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have. By using the App you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide voluntarily

  • Questions & feedback: If you submit a question through the in-app Q&A form, we collect the text of your question and, if you choose to provide it, your name. This information is stored so that we can publish answers for the benefit of all attendees.

Information collected automatically

  • Standard server logs: Our hosting provider (Supabase) may record standard technical data such as your IP address, request timestamps, and device type when the App communicates with our servers. This data is used solely for maintaining service reliability and diagnosing errors.

Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect location data, contacts, photos, or any other sensor data from your device.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display community questions and answers within the App.
  • To maintain, protect, and improve the App.
  • To respond to inquiries or issues you raise.

3. Data Storage & Security

Data is stored on servers operated by Supabase (hosted on AWS in the United States). We use industry-standard HTTPS encryption for data in transit. While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

4. Data Retention

Questions submitted through the App are retained for the duration of the celebration season and may be deleted afterward at our discretion. Server logs are retained for no more than 90 days.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • You may use the App without submitting any personal information — the schedule, map, and event details are viewable without providing data.
  • If you have submitted a question and would like it removed, contact us at the email below.
  • California residents and EU/EEA visitors have additional rights under CCPA and GDPR respectively; contact us to exercise them.

7. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase (database & hosting) — supabase.com/privacy
  • Apple App Store / Google Play (distribution) — see their respective privacy policies for information those platforms may collect independently.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
